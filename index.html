📜 CHEAT CODES Click any command to copy it
Command Description
/gold Adds 1,000,000 gold to your wallet instantly.
/money [amount] Sets your gold to the exact amount specified. ⚠ Sets total, does not add.
/debug renovate Opens Robin's renovation window to redesign your farmhouse.
/debug wall [Item ID] Replaces all wallpaper in your house with the specified item ID.
/debug floor [Item ID] Replaces all flooring in your house with the specified item ID.
/debug setupbigfarm Builds a fully stocked big farm — 3 coops, 2 barns, mill, stable, silo with animals & crops.
/debug houseupgrade [0-3] Upgrades your house. 0=Starter · 1=Kitchen · 2=Kids Room · 3=Cellar
/debug customization Opens the full character creator — change name, skin, hair & more anytime.
/debug dyepants Opens the pants color customization window.
/debug dyeshirt Opens the shirt color customization window.
/backpack 36 Fully upgrades your backpack to 36 slots instantly.
/debug cooking Unlocks every cooking recipe in the game.
/debug crafting Unlocks every crafting recipe in the game.
/debug levelup [skill] [level] Levels up a skill. 0=Farming · 1=Fishing · 2=Foraging · 3=Mining · 4=Combat
/debug befriend animals [#] Increases animal affection experience to given value (max +1000).
/animal [Name] Spawns a baby animal. Chicken · Cow · Duck · Goat · Pig · Rabbit · Sheep · Dinosaur · Void Chicken · Ostrich
/growanimals Makes all animals on the map instantly grow into adults.
/catchallfish Adds every fish to your Collections tab — marks all as caught.
/clearfishcaught Removes all fish from your Collections tab, resetting records.
/fishcaught [#] Sets your total fish caught record to the specified number.
/debug friendall Instantly maxes friendship with every villager in Pelican Town.
/characterinfo Shows the number of NPCs present on your current map.
/clearcharacters Removes all NPCs from the current map. ⚠ They return next day.
/dating [NPC name] Sets relationship status to boyfriend/girlfriend. e.g. /dating Haley
/marry [NPC name] Sets relationship status to husband/wife. e.g. /marry Emily
/makeex [NPC name] Ends your romantic relationship with your current spouse.
/debug pausetime Pauses or resumes in-game time. Enter again to toggle.
/debug time [0000] Sets in-game time (24h format). 0600=6AM · 1800=6PM
/debug addhour Adds one hour to the current in-game time.
/debug addminute Adds 10 minutes to the current in-game time.
/debug day [#] Changes to the specified day number within the current season.
/warphome Teleports your character instantly back to your farmhouse.
/warpshop [NPC Name] Teleports you to a specific NPC's shop. e.g. /warpshop Pierre
/debug speed [speed] [seconds] Gives a temporary speed buff. e.g. /debug speed 5 30
/debug clearinventory Removes all items from inventory, including unsellable ones. ⚠ Permanent!
/debug clearbackpack Clears and resets your backpack, removing all held items. ⚠ Permanent!
